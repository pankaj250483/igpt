I-GPT Ecosystem

At the heart of I-GPT ecosystem is the $CGI token, which is the utility token and key to accessing the marvels of the I-GPT Al models and our inclusive DAO.

Governance & Decision-Making

CGI token holders have the power to participate in the governance of the CardanoGPT ecosystem. They can propose and vote on changes, upgrades, and strategic decisions through a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO). This feature ensures that the community plays an active role in shaping the project's future direction.

Liquidity Provision & CGI Farming

CardanoGPT offers users the opportunity to participate in decentralized finance (DeFi) activities. By providing liquidity to the CGI token liquidity pool and engaging in yield farming, users can earn rewards and contribute to the liquidity of the CGI token.
